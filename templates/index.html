Do you want to make it safer to cross N 39th Street?

All you need to do is cross at Phinney.

Why Cross At Phinney?

Our goal is to get the Seattle Department of Transportation to conduct a study of pedestrian traffic at this intersection. If enough people cross at Phinney then measures will be taken to make crossing safer, such as better lighting or painting a crosswalk. Currently, people cross at many points along 39th which keeps the pedestrian counts too low at any given intersection to justify improvements. Find out more.
